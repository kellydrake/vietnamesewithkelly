Vietnamese Phrases

Here you'll find a few basic phrases in Vietnamese. If you need some pronunciation help, just click on the audio buttons.Interested in learning some vocabulary words? Check out the vocabulary page and test your learnings. Keep up the great work!

  • Hello = Xin chào
  • Yes/No = Dạ/Không
  • How are you? = Khỏe không?
  • Thank you = Cảm ơn
  • Are you hungry? = Đói bụng không?
  • Sorry = Xin lỗi
  • How much? = Bao nhiêu?
  • No problem = Không có gì
